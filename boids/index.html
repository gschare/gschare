Boids!

Boids are a model of bird flocking behavior. Like other artificial life simulations, they follow a few simple rules that result in complex emergent behavior. Plus, they're just fun to watch!

The rules are:

  • separation: steer to avoid other boids
  • alignment: steer to match the average heading of nearby boids
  • cohesion: steer towards the average position of nearby boids

If you're on a computer, the boids will avoid your cursor. If you're on mobile, you can tap to disrupt them!
