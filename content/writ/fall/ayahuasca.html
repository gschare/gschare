Background

Initial Memo

Hi folks!

I just wanted to address some concerns that were brought to me privately regarding our meeting earlier today. First, while this is a safe space for discussing our honest and real feelings about our relationship to psychoactive substances, it is absolutely inappropriate to encourage your peers to abuse while they are on a healing journey. This behavior will not be tolerated in this community and if it becomes clear that anyone has a harmful impact on the other members of the group, they will be asked to leave.

Second, I wanted to remind everyone that our sessions are about listening and hearing each other, not talking over one another or interrupting to share our own narratives, let alone disagreeing with the perspective of your peers. That being said, this morning was a great example of how to use “I” statements when sharing with the group, so applause for that!

Finally, if any of you were impacted by the asteroid impact, there are many resources available in the community to support you and your families. Please reach out to me directly or call our crisis helpline.

I understand that today was strange for many of us (myself included) but that does not mean we should abandon the progress we have all made as individuals and as a group. With these friendly notes in mind, I look forward to seeing you all next week.

As always, you can reach me at my personal number and at my office.

Happy Halloween!
Sam
