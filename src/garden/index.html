Gregory's Garden

A digital garden is a place to wander, plant seeds, whack weeds, tend and breathe. It is not a blog, portfolio, book, catalogue, or archive (though I do like those things). It is a celebration and appreciation of virtual space.

My garden is eclectic. You may find experiments, half-finished ideas, and poetry. You may find dead-ends and loops. You may find context or you may not. You may come back another day and find things have shifted, appeared, or disappeared.

Gardening is a process.
